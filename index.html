Voice Controlled Puzzle Game

Tips

Use your voice to control the game!

  • "Start game" - new game
  • "Left" - Move left
  • "Right" - Move right
  • "Rotate" - Rotate piece
  • "Down" - Move piece down
  • "Pause" - Pause
  • "Resume" - Resume

How to play

How the game works: Use your voice to control falling puzzle pieces. Commands like "left", "right", "rotate", and "down" move and rotate the pieces. Fill horizontal lines to clear them and score points.

How scoring is calculated: You earn 10 points for each line you clear. Try to clear as many lines as possible in a single move for a higher score!

How the game ends: The game ends when a new piece cannot be placed at the top of the board. Try to keep the board clear to keep playing!
